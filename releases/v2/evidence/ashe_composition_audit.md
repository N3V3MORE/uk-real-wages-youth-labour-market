# ASHE Composition Audit

This audit checks whether the 18-21 weekly-earnings result may be affected by worker or job composition. It compares all employees, full-time, part-time, male, female, and paid-hours rows where ASHE publishes them.

This is descriptive composition evidence, not causal evidence.

## Fields Checked

- Full-time and part-time median weekly rows by age group.
- Male and female median weekly rows by age group.
- Paid-hours movement from the ASHE hourly-pay and hours decomposition.
- Published ASHE number-of-jobs columns where raw Table 6 workbooks were available.

## Result

- 18-21: all-employee weekly pay changed by 25.66%; full-time 42.43%; part-time 48.2%; paid hours -20.4%.
  Full-time job share moved from 0.483 to 0.401; female job share moved from 0.517 to 0.507.
- 22-29: all-employee weekly pay changed by 32.54%; full-time 31.06%; part-time 42.28%; paid hours -0.53%.
  Full-time job share moved from 0.796 to 0.793; female job share moved from 0.491 to 0.504.
- 30-39: all-employee weekly pay changed by 33.16%; full-time 28.34%; part-time 44.98%; paid hours 0.0%.
  Full-time job share moved from 0.766 to 0.784; female job share moved from 0.480 to 0.488.
