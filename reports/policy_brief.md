# Policy Brief

## Headline Answer

Using ASHE age-specific median weekly earnings deflated by CPIH, the annual age-specific comparison currently runs from 2019 to 2025. The result should not be described as a 2026 age-specific wage result unless ASHE 2026 is available.

Age groups with real gains: 16-17, 22-29, 30-39, 40-49, 50-59, 60+.
Age groups with real losses: 18-21.
Weakest CPIH-deflated result: 18-21 at -1.81%.
Strongest CPIH-deflated result: 60+ at 10.26%.
The final summary table also includes a CPI-deflated sensitivity column `real_pct_change_cpi_deflator`.

## Limits

- ASHE is annual and age-specific.
- EARN01 is monthly but not age-specific.
- A05 SA is rolling three-monthly and seasonally adjusted.
- A05 SA is labelled by ONS as official statistics in development.
